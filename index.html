Just relax — discover Sortland

Here You'll find somthing close to your heart. Midnight sun in the summer or endless night in the winter. And one thing for sure — feeling and satisfaction of discovery!

The Blue City

Sortland is known as "the blue city" with a blue colour palette, buildings and street art, after an idea by local artist Bjørn Elvenes.

Aurora Hunt

Region is an ideal place to observe the Northern Lights. The region is located right within the Northern Lights oval and leading research into the Aurora happens here.

Nature Safari

The terrain varies from craggy alpine mountains to gentle, green hillsides. Here you will encounter wild and wonderful nature with a wow-factor that will take your breath away.

Hikers Paradise

The trails are easily accessible with beautiful views of fjords and arctic landscapes as far as the eye can see, regardless of which trail you choose.

Fastest way to visit us

Harstad-Narvik Airport, Evenes

Main airport in the region with wide range of destinations.

Stokmarknes Airport, Skagen

Local airport. Have flights with main region cites.

Sortland Port, Sortland

Regional port centrally located in Vesterålen and northern Norway. Regular cruise ships destination stop.
